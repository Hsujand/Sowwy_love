💔 A Little Apology 💔

Sometimes words remain trapped inside the heart.

Sometimes we realize too late that our actions hurt someone who never deserved that pain.

This little journey is simply my heart trying to say... "I'm sorry."

💌 First Letter

I know I cannot change what already happened.

I know some moments leave marks that cannot simply disappear.

But if I could, I would go back and choose better words, better actions, and a kinder heart.

🌙 Second Letter

I never wanted to become the reason behind your sadness.

If my mistakes made your heart heavy, then I truly regret them.

I cannot promise that I am perfect, but I can promise that I understand what I did wrong.

🥺 One Little Question

Will you let me try to make things right?

✨ Thank You ✨

Even listening to me means more than you know.

There is only one last thing I wish to ask.

Not to forget. Not to erase. Only to allow a chance for healing.

🌹 One Last Promise

Will you allow this story to become kinder than it was yesterday?

✨ Thank You ✨

Maybe scars stay.

Maybe memories stay.

But I hope one day this sadness becomes a gentle memory.

Thank you for hearing what my heart wanted to say.

💔 I Am Sorry 💔

🌙 I Will Learn 🌙

🌹 I Will Be Better 🌹

✨ And if someday you smile again, I will quietly be grateful. ✨
