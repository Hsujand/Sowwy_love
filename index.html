💔 A Little Apology 💔

Sometimes words remain trapped inside the heart.

Sometimes we realize too late that our actions hurt someone who never deserved that pain.

This little journey is simply my heart trying to say... "I'm sorry."

💌 First Letter

My Like Means...

I know that sometimes
I overthink the smallest changes.

I hold on to little words,
little moments,
and unknowingly turn them into questions.

I keep pulling at those thoughts
until they become heavier than they ever were...

and somehow,
the person who gets hurt the most
is the person I love the most.

That was never my intention.

Your Kishmishii only wanted to be
someone who makes you feel safe,
loved,
and special every single day.

I am truly sorry, my Love.

🌙 Second Letter

My sweet Gulaabii...

I know my actions
don't always show my heart.

But behind every unnecessary question
and every little fear...

is just a boy
who is scared of losing
the most beautiful thing in his life.

I want to be your comfort.
I want to be your peace.

I want to be the man
whose presence makes your days softer.

If my overthinking ever made you feel otherwise...

I am sorry.

I only ever wanted to love you better.

🥺 One Little Question

My Pulsiee...

Can we leave this little sadness behind
and hold each other a little closer?

Can we keep making memories,
keep laughing,
and keep choosing our love
over one difficult moment?

✨ Thank You ✨

Thank you...

Thank you for reading my heart.

Thank you for understanding
that sometimes your Kishmishii
gets lost in his own thoughts.

Your love has always been
one of the safest places I have ever known.

There is just one more thing
I want to ask you...

🌹 One Last Promise

Life will change.
Days will be different.
Sometimes we may even misunderstand each other.

But when those days come...

will you promise
that we won't let one bad moment
be bigger than our love?

Will we sit together,
talk together,
and find our way back to each other?

✨ Thank You ✨

My Like...

Thank you for staying.

I know your Kishmishii isn't perfect.

He thinks too much.
He worries too much.
And sometimes he lets his fears
speak louder than his heart.

But his heart has always belonged to you.

💔 I Am Sorry 💔

🌙 I Will Learn 🌙

🌹 I Will Be Better 🌹

💫 And my only wish is that...
no matter what life brings,
may my Like and her Kishmishii
always find home
in our love. ❤️
